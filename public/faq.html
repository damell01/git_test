Pricing & Billing
How much does a dumpster rental cost?
Pricing varies based on container size, rental duration, and the type of debris you're hauling. We use flat-rate pricing — the quote you receive includes delivery, pickup, and disposal with no hidden fees. Call us at (251) 333-4444 for a fast, free quote specific to your project.
Are there any hidden fees?
Never. We're big believers in transparent, flat-rate pricing. The number we quote you is the number on your bill. The only exceptions are overage fees if you exceed the weight limit (we'll discuss that upfront), or fees for prohibited materials — which we also tell you about before you book.
How long is my rental period?
Standard rental periods are typically 7–14 days. Need more time? We offer extended rentals — just let us know when you book or give us a call before your rental expires. We work around your project schedule, not the other way around.
Do you charge extra for heavy materials like concrete or dirt?
Heavy materials like concrete, brick, and dirt have weight limits that differ from standard debris. We'll discuss this with you upfront when you book. For heavy material loads, we recommend our smaller containers (10 yard) to stay within weight limits and avoid overage fees.
Delivery & Pickup
How fast can you deliver a dumpster?
In most cases, we can deliver same-day or next-morning across our primary service area in Baldwin County and Mobile. Call us by noon for the best chance of a same-day drop. We'll always give you a specific delivery window so you know exactly when to expect us.
Where will you place the dumpster?
We'll place it wherever is most convenient for your project — driveway, yard, alley, or parking lot. Just let us know your preference when you book. If you want it on a public street or road, some municipalities require a permit; we can help advise on that.
Do I need to be home for delivery or pickup?
Nope! As long as there's a clear, accessible spot for us to place (or retrieve) the dumpster, you don't need to be present. Just leave us clear instructions when you book and we'll take care of the rest.
How do I schedule a pickup when I'm done?
Simple — just call us at (251) 333-4444 when you're ready. We'll schedule the pickup at your convenience, usually within 24–48 hours. There's no extra charge for pickup — it's included in your flat rate.
What Can I Throw Away?
What materials are accepted?
We accept a wide range of materials: household junk and furniture, construction debris (wood, drywall, flooring), yard waste, roofing shingles, appliances (without freon), mattresses, and general trash. When in doubt, just ask us — we're happy to clarify.
What materials are NOT allowed?
Prohibited materials include: hazardous waste, paint and solvents, asbestos, tires, batteries, propane tanks, refrigerants/freon, medical waste, and flammable liquids. These require specialized disposal — we can point you toward the right resources for these materials.
Can I put electronics in the dumpster?
TVs, computers, and other electronics (e-waste) typically cannot go in roll-off dumpsters due to environmental regulations. Many municipalities have e-waste drop-off programs — we're happy to point you in the right direction.
General Questions
Which dumpster size do I need?
As a general guide: a 10-yard is perfect for small cleanouts; a 20-yard works for most residential renovations and is our most popular size; a 30-yard handles large renovations and commercial cleanouts; a 40-yard is for major demolitions and large commercial projects. Not sure? Call us and describe your project — we'll size you right in under a minute.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, Trash Panda Roll-Offs is fully licensed and insured for both residential and commercial work throughout our service area. We're happy to provide proof of insurance for any commercial or contractor project.
Do you offer contractor pricing?
Yes! We work with many contractors throughout Baldwin County and Mobile. If you're running multiple jobs or need regular service, give us a call to discuss contractor rates and preferred scheduling options.
Can I keep the dumpster longer than my rental period?
Absolutely. We offer flexible rental extensions. Just call us before your rental period ends and we'll add time at a prorated daily rate. We'd rather work with you than leave you in a bind mid-project.
